FAQ’s

What is GlobeOne?

GlobeOne is a financial community that facilitates the full-service “GO Package”. The benefits include a checking account with a debit card and credit line, a security account to help save for the future, and mobile member-to-member domestic and international transfers without the standard fee charge. It also provides an income opportunity through the personal and global communities, which is funded by the member banks.

How do I get started?

Enter your email, click “Join Us”, get approved and get started.

Without branches, how do I get money into GlobeOne?

There are a number of options. The easiest way is through direct deposit into your account with a member bank, and the member bank may allow you to transfer funds electronically from an account at another financial institution, deposit a check by taking a photo of it with your mobile phone (if your member bank provides these services) or deposit by mail. You could also deposit to your account with a member bank by getting a money order at a local grocery or convenience store, and then depositing it with the mobile app (if your member bank provides this service).

Does GlobeOne Charge Fees?

We're not fans of unnecessary fees at GlobeOne. With the exception of the monthly $9.95 membership fee, GlobeOne charges no other fees.

If you don’t profit from fees, how do you make money?

Although we love technology, we do not knock tradition. In the past, banks and financial institutions made most of their profits off interest. With GlobeOne, member banks return 50% of interest to the Personal and Global Communities. GlobeOne is paid entirely through members’ $9.95 flat monthly fees.

Who can use GlobeOne?

Any person who meets the minimum requirements of the Member Banks of Globe One.

What Features do you provide?

The GlobeOne member banks work to provide your banking needs in one place; a checking account with no minimum balance, a debit card , a security account, and a line of credit. In addition, members can participate in the personal and global community with income opportunities funded by the member banks. You'll be able to pay bills by mobile phone or online, direct deposit your checks (as offered by the member banks) and transfer money from member to member domestically and internationally. The “GO Package” provides you with the necessary financial tools you will need to assist in enhancing your financial well-being.

Does GlobeOne offer bill pay?

If your member bank provides online bill paying services, you can use your GlobeOne account to utilize them.

Is GlobeOne available in other countries outside the United States?

Yes, we are working to revolutionize financial freedom worldwide.

Is there an inactivity fee?

GlobeOne does not charge any added fees other than the monthly $9.95 fee for membership.

Can I get a card if I have bad credit or no credit history?

GlobeOne and its Member Banks make every effort to provide accounts as acceptable by law. While there is never a guarantee of an account approval, every effort is made to accommodate applications wherever possible and in compliance with law.
