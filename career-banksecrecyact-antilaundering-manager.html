Bank Secrecy Act and Anti-Laundering Manager

We are looking for professionals who have experience and interest in financial products and services. Such candidates have worked on large e-commerce products, banking applications, and credit/debit card processing or issuing institutions, ACH/EDI transactions, as well as more general experience dealing with financial data. Such candidates have at least 5, and preferably 10 or more years of relevant industry knowledge. They have at least a BS degree and relevant certifications (PMP, ITIL, TOGAF, and others), especially as applied in the Finance Vertical.

Administration of the client's enterprise compliance program and specific projects, such as monitoring, testing, auditing, performing risk assessments, and providing training.

General Duties and Responsibilities

  • Administers compliance programs for clients.
  • Manages and/or conducts institution-wide and/or product-specific compliance reviews and assessments for clients and complexities across all compliance topics, including: BSA/AML, lending, deposits, CRA and Fair Lending, and Operations.
  • Prepares clear, concise, and accurate reports of findings and recommendations.
  • Maintains an outstanding knowledge of current federal laws and regulations.
  • Assists clients with enhancing, developing, and implementing effective compliance controls including policies, procedures, and processes.
  • Advises product managers of compliance requirements for new product enhancements.
  • Monitors, interprets and communicates regulatory changes that impact existing business activities.
  • Develops, coordinates and participates in compliance educational and training programs.
  • Presents compliance updates to executive management and boards of directors.
  • Develops, leads, manages and motivates staff, as assigned.
  • Develops articles, web scripts, seminar content and other materials on relevant compliance topics to assist clients in understanding specific compliance regulations.
  • Presents compliance seminars, web-casts and other compliance-oriented presentations to internal and external clients.

Education Requirements

BS, MS preferred. Business administration or related discipline preferred.

Experience, Knowledge, And Abilities

Prefer 7-to-10 years experience in one or more of the following roles:

  • Regulatory Examiner
  • Compliance Consultant
  • Financial Institution Compliance Officer / Manager / Auditor

Compliance-related professional certification is preferred but not required, examples:

  • Certified Regulatory Compliance Manager (CRCM)
  • Certified Financial Services Auditor (CFSA)
  • Certified Anti-Money Laundering Specialist (CAMS)
