Employer FAQ’s

Who is eligible to open a GlobeOne account?

All employees are entitled to apply for a GlobeOne account. We request a minimal amount of required documentation to ensure both the security of the employer and the employee. Credit checks, social security cards, and resident status are not pre-requisites. We work to make this a “true” benefit for the organization as a whole and to ensure that everyone is encouraged to apply.

How does an employer enroll employees into GlobeOne?

GlobeOne offers several options for employers to make the on-boarding and enrollment of employees simple, easy and effective. We understand both time and personnel constraints in any organization, so we strive to make every interaction with GlobeOne as pleasant and productive as possible.

How do my employees access their account information?

New account holders will receive all necessary account information, including instructions for logging into their account, online. They can also access account information via their statement, or by calling our customer service line 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

How can I confirm that my employees' application information has been received/reviewed?

We will send information directly to an employee once their account is opened. As an employer, if you wish to conﬁrm that application information has been received and processed, you may contact our employer service line 24 hours a day, 7 days a week at the number listed in the “Contact Us” section of the website.

How long does it take to an open a GlobeOne account?

Once data is received, we will process the application within 24 hours and most accounts are opened at that time. If the account information cannot be veriﬁed according to the USA Patriot Act guidelines, we will contact the employee to request further veriﬁcation prior to opening the account.

What Communication materials will my employees receive from GlobeOne?

Within 7 – 10 business days after account opening, your employee will receive a welcome kit with a signature card, along with all necessary materials to access and use their new account. A description of each of these mailings is included within your employer guide, accessible on the Website. Please note that account materials are sent separately in generic envelopes for security reasons. It would be helpful to advise employees to check their mail carefully for 2 weeks after opening their accounts.

What happens to an employee’s GlobeOne account if they are no longer employed by the company?

Former employees may continue to retain the GlobeOne membership and use their account as usual. The membership always remains in the employee's name for their personal use, regardless of their employment status.
