Employer FAQ’s

Who is eligible to open a GlobeOne account?

All employees are entitled to apply for a GlobeOne account. We request the minimum amount of legally required documentation to ensure both the security of the employer and the employee. We work to make this a “true” benefit for the organization as a whole and to ensure that everyone is encouraged to apply.

How does an employer enroll employees into GlobeOne?

GlobeOne offers several options for employers to make the on-boarding and enrollment of employees simple, easy and effective. We understand both time and personnel constraints in any organization, so we strive to make every interaction with GlobeOne as pleasant and productive as possible.

How do my employees access their account information?

New account holders will receive all necessary bank account information either by mail or logging into their account, online. They can also access account information through their member bank, or by calling the customer service line noted on the back of their debit card for assistance pursuant to the bank’s terms and conditions.

How can I confirm that my employees' application information has been received/reviewed?

Information will be sent directly to an employee once their account is opened.

How long does it take to an open a GlobeOne account?

The accounts are processed by a member bank. All efforts are taken to expedite the process. If the account information cannot be veriﬁed according to the USA Patriot Act or other applicable regulatory guidelines, or if questions exist, the member bank will contact the employee to request further veriﬁcation.

What Communication materials will my employees receive from GlobeOne?

The member bank will follow its bank policies and procedures to communicate with the employee regarding all appropriate banking documents. A description, and as applicable, an example of each of these banking documents is provided within your employer guide. Please note that account materials are sent by the member bank to the address provided by the employee. It would be helpful to advise employees to check their mail carefully for 2 weeks after opening their accounts.

What happens to an employee’s GlobeOne account if they are no longer employed by the company?

Former employees may continue to retain the GlobeOne membership and use their bank account as usual. The membership always remains in the employee's name for their personal use, regardless of their employment status.
