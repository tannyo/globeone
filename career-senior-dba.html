Senior DBA

Senior DBA is responsible for set-up and administration of our clustered mission-critical, multi-country database environment. MySQL NDB Carrier Grade Cluster or Percona XTRADB Cluster with Galera Monitoring services on Linux servers using SNMP, Cacti, Zabbix and IPMonitor.

Responsibilities

  • Preferred experience in building, configuring, and maintaining the database in a SaaS or E-commerce business.
  • In-depth knowledge of horizontal database splitting, partitioning and data sharing is required.
  • Experience with large data sets with at least with 500M rows and over 2TB in size.
  • Lead ownership of the MySQL/Postgre/SQL Server databases for production, staging and development.
  • Monitor databases for problems and diagnose issues.
  • Work with Engineering and IT to maintain a scalable, reliable, and robust database environment.
  • Work with developers on performance tuning, query optimization, index tuning.
  • Very strong experience in replication, clustering, tuning, sizing and proactive monitoring.
  • Knowledge of creating, maintaining and supporting database schemas.
  • Participate in the creation of SQL development standards while adhering to policies, methodologies, standards and guidelines.
  • Must have experience in backup, recovery and upgrade procedure and process.
  • Create and refine database complex queries, indexes, stored procedures and data mining.
  • Proactively monitor the database systems to ensure secure services with minimum downtime.
  • In-depth knowledge of MySQL InnoDB and MyISAM.
  • Escalation point for 24/7 support for all DB related incidents; on-call pager duty is required for this role.

Skills

  • Senior DBA with minimum 8 years experience in database engineering, administration and support.
  • Advanced knowledge of MySQL and/or Percona and related suite of tools.
  • Experience with large-scale, complex distributed Linux, Unix and Windows applications.
  • Experience in performance tuning and database monitoring tools.
  • Working knowledge of hardware including RAID, Shared Disk, SAN, multi-processor architecture, networking with database and application connectivity.
  • Ability to develop complex databases to support enterprise level, high volume business applications.
  • Ability to build, maintain and support complex SQL stored procedures in collaboration with other IT or Engineering staff.
  • Ability to participate and/or lead the efforts to gather requirements analysis for new projects.
  • Ability to identify security requirements, including creation of new roles and/or addition to existing roles for a given line of business.
  • Responsible for meeting all assigned/agreed upon target dates for projects as well as individual tasks.
  • Ability to handle numerous and conflicting priorities in a professional manner.
  • High degree of independence and exceptional work ethic.
  • Expectation to be challenged.
