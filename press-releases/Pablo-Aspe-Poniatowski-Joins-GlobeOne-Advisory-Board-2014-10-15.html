Pablo Aspe Poniatowski Joins GlobeOne Advisory Board

Santa Monica, CA – October 14, 2014 – GlobeOne today announced that Pablo Aspe Poniatowski, Managing Director of Casa De Bolsa and General Manager of Wealth Management at Scotiabank Inverlat S.A., has joined the GlobeOne Advisory Board. “I am a strong believer in GlobeOne’s vision of giving every human being their natural-born right to control their financial future. I am also confident that the leadership team at GlobeOne has the capability, the tenacity and the perseverance to manifest their vision with the appropriate support and commitment from the financial community”, said Mr. Aspe Poniatowski in a recent interview announcing his appointment.

Mr. Aspe Poniatowski has over 20 years of experience in the banking and financial sector beginning as an executive at Inverlat in 1993 as head of the Factoring subsidiary. He then moved to Scotiabank International Banking Group in Toronto, Canada before being asked to return to Mexico where he spearheaded the development and expansion of their portfolio of global banking products. During his time at Scotiabank Inverlat, Aspe Poniatowski opened over 300 new branches and implemented a strong customer-focused service culture that was widely applauded and garnered tremendous success in the challenging but rapidly growing Mexican market.

“We are honored to have Mr. Aspe Poniatowski join our mission to re-envision the global financial landscape”, said Michael Wolper, GlobeOne member and Chief Marketing Officer. “We are creating a truly modern economic model based on historically successful financial roots and I’m confident with the pioneering guidance of Mr. Aspe Poniatowski, GlobeOne will have far greater impact and success. Not only does Mr. Aspe Poniatowski’s background make him uniquely qualified to offer strategic guidance, but he also shares in our belief that true freedom comes from financial security – and that’s where we’re all headed together, as a community. We look forward to an exciting future together”.
