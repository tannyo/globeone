GlobeOne Appoints Stephen C. Landry Chief Operating and Technology Officer

Santa Monica, California – December 3, 2014 – GlobeOne, a new technology company that has developed a mobile-based financial platform designed to provide access to banking products for the underserved and self-banked millennials globally, today announced the appointment of Stephen C. Landry as Chief Operating and Technology Officer.

Stephen joins GlobeOne with over 30 years of experience in the financial services industry, combining both consulting and industry positions. He was most recently a partner with Atlanta-based consulting firm Bank Solutions Group and was president of the payments industry consulting firm, LGC. Stephen also served as executive vice president of Providian Financial and spent more than 15 years as senior vice president of the consumer banking and consumer credit divisions of Fleet/Boston and Wells Fargo Bank.

In his role as Chief Operating and Technology Officer for GlobeOne, Stephen will focus on the development and delivery of the GlobeOne mobile-based financial platform, set to launch in early 2015.

Working with a network of banks worldwide, the GlobeOne mobile app will provide an easy, engaging and intuitive suite of financial services, including paperless checking, a security savings account, a line of credit and unlimited instant domestic and international transfers, and an evolutionary global sharing-economy based model called SocialBoost, all for a flat monthly fee of $9.95. Stephen’s responsibilities include overseeing the development team creating GlobeOne’s unique, proprietary and multi-lingual application. Stephen will also work closely with the company’s bank partners, in all regions, to ensure the successful integration of the GlobeOne platform.

“We are excited to have someone of Stephen’s experience and expertise join the GlobeOne executive team,” says Michael Wolper, Chief Marketing Officer at GlobeOne. “Stephen brings a tremendous amount of knowledge in business strategy, process, technology and consolidation efforts across the consumer payments value chain. I am confident Stephen’s industry expertise and leadership track record will provide significant value in helping GlobeOne successfully launch our mobile-based financial platform early next year.”

Stephen comments, “I am very pleased to be joining an organization dedicated to breaking down financial barriers and offering everyone access to simple, affordable financial tools regardless of where they live or how much they make. I look forward to working closely with the team to enhance the delivery and capability of the GlobeOne platform to our community worldwide.”
